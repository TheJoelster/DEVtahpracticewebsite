So what are quadratics?

Quadratic functions have a degree of 2, meaning one term with x in it will be raised to the power of two. Displayed graphically, this forms a bowl shape. There are many ways to write a quadratic function, but for the purpose of this lesson, we are going to use something called standard form, which is written as:.

{y=ax^2+bx+c}
